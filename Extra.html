Full Scholarship

I had a passion for sports at a young age. I tried tennis, basketball, volleyball, table tennis and badminton. I pursued a badminton career by self-training and joining tournaments.

I applied to Mapua University and got accepted. I received a full athletic scholarship in Badminton during my collegiate years. Not everyone received a full scholarship but I was one of the best in my team.

Student Athlete

Mapua is one of the only schools in the Philippines that have 4 semesters (Quarterm) per year, creating a hectic schedule for a student athlete.

There was a daily training schedule at 7am and an absent period of 2 weeks for the NCAA. Time management was crucial for my survival.

Team captain

I was team captain of the Men's Badminton team during 2021-2022. I was appointed team captain, not as the best player but the player who brings the most value to the team and leads by example.

I learned how to manage and inspire people effectively. The experience had embedded multiple life lessons and established meaningful relationships.
